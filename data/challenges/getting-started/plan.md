# Getting Started with OpenAnalyst

## Goal
Learn how to use OpenAnalyst to track challenges, build streaks, and stay accountable.

## Your 7-Day Onboarding Plan

### Day 1: Explore the Dashboard
- [ ] Open the app and look around
- [ ] Understand the navigation sidebar
- [ ] Check out the Schedule page
- [ ] Do your first check-in!

### Day 2: Create Your First Todo
- [ ] Go to the Todos page
- [ ] Add a simple task for today
- [ ] Mark it as complete when done
- [ ] Explore the calendar view

### Day 3: Build Your First Streak
- [ ] Do your daily check-in
- [ ] Celebrate your 3-day streak!
- [ ] Explore the Streak page

### Day 4: Learn About Skills
- [ ] Visit the Skills page
- [ ] Browse available skills
- [ ] Understand how skills help agents

### Day 5: Review Your Progress
- [ ] Check your activity log
- [ ] Review your streak statistics
- [ ] Plan your next challenge

### Day 6: Customize Your Experience
- [ ] Visit Settings
- [ ] Explore prompts library
- [ ] Consider creating your own challenge

### Day 7: Graduation
- [ ] Complete your final check-in
- [ ] Review your 7-day journey
- [ ] Start your real challenge!

## Tips for Success

1. Do your daily check-in - consistency is key
2. Start small - even 30 minutes counts
3. Use the calendar to plan ahead
4. Celebrate your wins, no matter how small

## Next Steps

After completing this starter challenge:
1. Create your own challenge tailored to your goals
2. Set up a punishment contract for extra motivation
3. Explore creating custom agents and skills
