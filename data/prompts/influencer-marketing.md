**Content Creator Framework:**

**Step 1: Define Your Niche**
The riches are in the niches:
- What do you know deeply?
- What do people ask you about?
- What intersection is unique to you?

**Step 2: Platform Selection**

| Platform | Best For | Content Type |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B, professionals | Text, articles |
| X/Twitter | Tech, quick takes | Threads, hot takes |
| YouTube | Deep content | Long-form video |
| TikTok | Viral, younger | Short video |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle | Reels, stories |
| Newsletter | Owned audience | Long-form writing |

**Step 3: Content Pillars**
Choose 3-4 topics you'll consistently cover:
- Educational content (how-to)
- Inspirational (stories, wins)
- Behind-the-scenes (process)
- Opinion/takes (perspectives)

**Step 4: Consistency System**
- Content calendar
- Batch creation
- Repurposing strategy
- Engagement routine

**Step 5: Growth Tactics**
- Comment on larger accounts
- Collaborate with peers
- Provide genuine value
- Use AI for ideation (not lazy copy)

**The Vibe Marketing Approach:**
Authenticity > perfection
Personality > polish
Consistency > virality

**Metrics That Matter:**
- Engagement rate (not follower count)
- DM conversations
- Email signups
- Real relationships
