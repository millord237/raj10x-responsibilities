**Project Initiation Framework:**

**Step 1: Define the Vision**
- What is this project?
- Why does it matter to you?
- What does success look like?

**Step 2: Scope Definition**
Be specific about boundaries:
- What's IN scope?
- What's OUT of scope?
- What are the deliverables?

**Step 3: Resource Assessment**
What do you have?
- Time: {{daily_hours}} hours daily available
- Skills: What can you do?
- Tools: What do you have access to?
- Support: Who can help?

**Step 4: Timeline Planning**
- Target completion date?
- Key milestones along the way?
- Check-in points?

**Step 5: Risk Identification**
What could go wrong?
- Potential blockers
- Dependencies on others
- Skill gaps
- Time conflicts

**Step 6: First Actions**
The project starts with ACTION:
- What's the very first task?
- When will you do it?
- How will you track progress?
