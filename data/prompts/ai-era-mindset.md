**Thriving in the AI Era:**

**1. The New Reality**
AI is not replacing you. A person using AI well will replace someone who isn't.

**Key Shifts:**
- From memorization → to curation & synthesis
- From doing → to directing & reviewing
- From expertise → to judgment & creativity
- From lone genius → to AI-human collaboration

**2. Skills That Matter More Now**

**Amplified by AI:**
- Critical thinking (evaluating AI output)
- Creativity (ideation AI can't replicate)
- Communication (explaining complex ideas)
- Emotional intelligence (human connection)
- Domain expertise (knowing what to ask)

**New Essential Skills:**
- Prompt engineering
- AI tool fluency
- Data literacy
- Systems thinking

**3. The AI Collaboration Framework**

**PREP Method:**
- **P**repare: Define your goal clearly
- **R**equest: Craft effective prompts
- **E**valuate: Review and refine output
- **P**ersonalize: Add your unique value

**4. Career Future-Proofing**

| Risk Level | Action |
|------------|--------|
| High risk of automation | Learn to supervise AI in that task |
| Medium risk | Become the human-AI bridge |
| Low risk | Enhance with AI tools |

**5. Daily AI Integration**

- Morning: AI brief on industry news
- Work: AI for research, drafts, analysis
- Learning: AI tutoring and explanation
- Evening: AI for planning tomorrow

**6. Mindset Mantras**

- "AI is my junior colleague, not my replacement"
- "My judgment + AI capability = superpower"
- "Adapt weekly, not yearly"
- "Build with AI, not against it"
