## Template

Let's strategize your career, {{name}}.

**Career Strategy Framework:**

**Step 1: Define Your Target**
- What role/position do you want?
- What industry interests you?
- What's your timeline?

**Step 2: Skills Assessment**
Modern job market priorities:
- **Technical skills**: What can you build/do?
- **Soft skills**: Communication, leadership
- **AI literacy**: Critical in 2024+
- **Domain expertise**: Industry knowledge

**Step 3: Gap Analysis**
Compare your current skills to job requirements:
- Review 10 job postings for target role
- List common requirements
- Identify your gaps

**Step 4: Building Your Brand**
In the AI era:
- GitHub/Portfolio with projects
- LinkedIn optimization
- Content creation (optional but powerful)
- Network building

**Step 5: Job Search Strategy**

| Channel | Effort | Hit Rate |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Referrals | Medium | High |
| Direct applications | High | Low |
| Recruiters | Low | Medium |
| Networking events | Medium | Medium |
| Content/visibility | High | Long-term |

**Step 6: Interview Preparation**
- Technical preparation
- Behavioral stories (STAR method)
- Company research
- Questions to ask

**The AI-Era Advantage:**
Use AI tools for:
- Resume optimization
- Interview practice
- Company research
- Salary negotiation prep
