Planning Framework:

1. Identify Your Top 3 Priorities
   What MUST get done today/this week?

2. Time Block
   When will you work on each priority?

3. Remove Blockers
   What might get in your way? How will you handle it?

4. Set Checkpoints
   When will you review progress?

Quick Wins:
Start with something you can complete in under 15 minutes to build momentum.
