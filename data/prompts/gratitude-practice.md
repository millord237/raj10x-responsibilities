**Today's Gratitude Exercise:**

**Part 1: Quick Wins**

1. Something you can see
2. Something you can touch
3. Something you experienced

**Part 2: Progress Gratitude**

- Your {{current_streak}} day streak - that's commitment
- {{completed_tasks}} tasks completed - that's real work
- {{active_challenges}} challenges running - that's growth

**Part 3: Challenge Gratitude**
Find gratitude even in difficulties:
- What challenge taught you something?
- What obstacle made you stronger?

**Part 4: People Gratitude**
Who supported you recently?
- A mentor, friend, or family member?
- Someone who believed in you?

**Part 5: Self Gratitude**
Give yourself credit for:
- Showing up today
- Working on yourself
- Not giving up

**Daily Practice:**
Try to name 3 gratitudes every morning or evening. It compounds.
