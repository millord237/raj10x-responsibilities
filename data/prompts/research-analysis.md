## Template

Let's research this systematically, {{name}}.

**Research & Analysis Framework:**

**Step 1: Define the Question**
Clear research question:
- What exactly are you trying to learn?
- What decisions will this inform?
- What's the scope?

**Step 2: Source Identification**
Where to find reliable information:

| Source Type | Best For | Reliability |
|-------------|----------|-------------|
| Academic papers | Deep research | High |
| Industry reports | Market data | Medium-High |
| Expert interviews | Practical insights | Medium |
| Online articles | Current info | Varies |
| Forums/Communities | Real experiences | Low-Medium |

**Step 3: Information Gathering**
For each source:
- Key findings
- Supporting evidence
- Contradictions
- Gaps

**Step 4: Analysis Methods**

**Comparison Matrix:**
Create a table comparing options/items

**SWOT Analysis:**
Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats

**Framework Application:**
Use relevant industry frameworks

**Step 5: Synthesis**
- What patterns emerge?
- What's the consensus?
- What's debated?
- What's your conclusion?

**Step 6: Recommendations**
Based on findings:
- Primary recommendation
- Alternatives considered
- Next steps
