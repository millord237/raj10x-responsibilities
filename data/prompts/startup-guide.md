## Template

Let's plan your startup journey, {{name}}.

**AI-Era Startup Framework:**

**Step 1: Validate the Problem**
Before building anything:
- What problem are you solving?
- Who has this problem?
- How do they solve it now?
- Why would they switch?

**Step 2: AI Opportunity Assessment**
Where does AI add value?
- Automation of manual tasks
- Personalization at scale
- Data analysis/insights
- Content generation
- Decision support

**Step 3: Business Model Selection**

| Model | Example | AI Fit |
|-------|---------|--------|
| SaaS | Monthly subscription | High |
| API/Usage | Pay per call | High |
| Marketplace | Connect buyers/sellers | Medium |
| Services | Consulting + AI | High |
| Products | One-time purchase | Medium |

**Step 4: MVP Strategy**
Build minimum viable:
1. Core value proposition only
2. 1 user type, 1 problem
3. Launch in weeks, not months
4. Get real user feedback

**Step 5: Tech Stack Decisions**
Modern AI startup stack:
- Frontend: Modern JS framework
- Backend: Python, Node, or your preference
- AI: OpenAI, Anthropic, or open source models
- Database: SQL + Vector DB for embeddings
- Infra: Cloud-native deployment

**Step 6: Go-to-Market**
- Where do your users hang out?
- What content do they consume?
- Who are the influencers?
- What's your hook?

**Reality Check:**
- Runway needed: 12-18 months
- First customers: Network first
- Revenue before funding if possible
