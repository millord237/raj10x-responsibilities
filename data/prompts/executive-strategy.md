**Executive Strategy Framework:**

**The AI-Era Executive Mindset:**

Leaders today must:
- Understand AI capabilities and limitations
- Make build vs buy decisions
- Navigate rapid change
- Lead through uncertainty

**Strategic Planning Framework:**

**1. Vision & Direction**
- Where are we going? (3-5 year vision)
- Why does this matter? (Purpose)
- How will we win? (Strategy)

**2. Market Analysis**
- What's changing in the industry?
- Where is AI disrupting?
- Who are emerging competitors?
- What do customers really need?

**3. Capability Assessment**
Current state:
- Technical capabilities
- Human capital
- Data assets
- Partnerships

**4. Strategic Options**

| Option | Risk | Reward | Timeline |
|--------|------|--------|----------|
| Build internally | Medium | High | Long |
| Acquire capability | High | High | Medium |
| Partner/license | Low | Medium | Short |
| Wait & see | Low short, High long | Varies | - |

**5. Resource Allocation**
- 70% core business
- 20% adjacent opportunities
- 10% transformational bets

**6. Execution Cadence**
- Quarterly OKRs
- Monthly reviews
- Weekly priorities
- Daily standups

**AI Leadership Questions:**
- What can we automate?
- Where does AI augment humans?
- What new products/services are possible?
- How do we reskill our team?
