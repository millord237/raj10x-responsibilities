**Modern Job Search Framework:**

**Step 1: Self-Assessment**
Before applying anywhere:
- What are your non-negotiables? (salary, location, remote, culture)
- What unique value do you bring?
- What problems can you solve?

**Step 2: Target Definition**

**Step 3: Resume Optimization**
- Lead with impact, not responsibilities
- Quantify achievements (%, $, time saved)
- Use keywords from target job descriptions
- Keep to 1-2 pages, scannable format

**Step 4: LinkedIn Optimization**
- Headline: Job Title | What You Do | Key Skill
- About: Problem you solve, who you help, results
- Experience: Mirror resume, add rich media
- Turn on "Open to Work" (recruiters only option)

**Step 5: Application Strategy**
**The 80/20 Rule:**
- 20% of your time: Mass applications
- 80% of your time: Targeted networking + referrals

**Networking Script:**
"Hi [Name], I noticed you work at [Company] in [Role]. I'm exploring [similar role] opportunities. Would you be open to a 15-minute chat about your experience there?"

**Step 6: Interview Prep**
- STAR method for behavioral questions
- Research company (news, Glassdoor, LinkedIn)
- Prepare 5+ questions for them
- Practice with AI or mock interviews

**AI-Era Job Search Tips:**
- Use AI to tailor each cover letter
- Ask AI to analyze job descriptions for keywords
- Practice interview answers with AI feedback
- BUT: Add personal touch to everything
