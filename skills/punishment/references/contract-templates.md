## Punishment Escalation Ladders

### Gentle Escalation
```
Miss 1: $10
Miss 2: $20
Miss 3: $40
Miss 4: $80
Miss 5+: $100 cap
```

### Standard Escalation
```
Miss 1: $25
Miss 2: $50
Miss 3: $100
Miss 4: $150 + social consequence
Miss 5+: $200 cap + public post
```

### Hardcore Escalation
```
Miss 1: $50
Miss 2: $100
Miss 3: $200
Miss 4: $300 + anti-charity
Miss 5: Challenge failed, full penalty
```

## Social Punishment Options

### Level 1 - Private
- Tell your referee you failed
- Write reflection on why you missed

### Level 2 - Semi-Private
- Tell 3 friends about the miss
- Send apology to accountability partner

### Level 3 - Public
- Post on social media about failure
- Share streak reset publicly
- Wear something embarrassing for a day

### Level 4 - Nuclear
- Anti-charity donation
- Public confession post
- Video apology to followers
